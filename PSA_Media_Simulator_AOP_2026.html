How this works. The full-year media + influencer budget ($94K = $66K media advertising + $28K influencer, per the AOP) and Shopify target ($230,050) are the revised AOP. H1 2026 is sunk: actual Shopify net revenue and media + influencer spend (AOP "revenue all", Jan–May; June = YTD average; influencer ~½ of FY spent in H1) are removed, leaving the H2 budget and H2 target. Each model then projects how much revenue the remaining H2 budget produces at a given efficiency — Models 1 & 2 via a blended MER (revenue ÷ media spend), Model 3 via PSA's 1.5× acquisition ROAS plus the AOP organic base. Full-year achievable = H1 actual + H2 projected. Budget covers paid media + influencers; programmatic display (RTB House + Criteo) is excluded (stopped). Directional planning aid, not a guarantee.
